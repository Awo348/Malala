Why I chose her as my Hero

Right from when I was a teenager, I read her book and I was inspired by how focused and smart she is. I got interested in her empathy to help and strive for a better life and not limiting herself to the contemporary world of she's just a girl and as such, she shouldn’t be useful. She made me realize that as a woman, we are smarter and stronger than we think and that everyone deserves education and awareness. She made me realize that you don’t have to accept the society view about yourself. Her life story really inspired me and how far she has come.
