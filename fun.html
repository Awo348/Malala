Fun Fact on Malala Yousafzai

Malala's advocacy for girls' education has made her a global icon, and her new initiative is sure to have a significant impact on the lives of millions of girls around the world. By providing direct support, developing innovative educational programs, and advocating for policies that support girls' education, the Malala Fund is making a real difference in the fight for girls' rights.

The Malala Fund's work will focus on three key areas:

Direct Support to Schools

The Fund will provide funding and technical assistance to schools in remote and underserved communities to improve their infrastructure, teacher training, and access to resources.

Educational programs

The Fund will develop and implement innovative educational programs that are tailored to the specific needs of girls in these communities.

Advocacy

The Fund will work with governments, policymakers, and other stakeholders to promote policies and legislation that support girls' education, including increased funding for education, gender-sensitive education curricula, and policies that protect girls from violence.
